Ali Abdaal 3种日记写法信息整理

介绍里说的再放一遍。

原视频：Ali Abdaal - ✍️改变人生的3种日记写法｜日记改变人生的底层逻辑，以及5个简单的日记练习方法。

以下归纳仅作个人使用，而且可能混杂着自己的理解（臆想），视频里有详细举例和示范，更有助于理解。

由于原视频是英文，自己做笔记时会中英文混杂，虽然我也不喜欢这样…对这篇概括来说更适合的应该是纯英文，但显然对我有一定难度，就只能别扭地看了。

这个视频是在介绍记日记的3种level。

Level 1: Write down what are things you’ve done today.

Strategy 1: Homework For Life

At the end of every day, you ask yourself, what was the most storyworthy thing that happened to me today.

用大概几句话记下来，并且用这些话回忆，有哪件是今天最值得可以在5m分钟内说出来的故事。

Strategy 2: Morning Pages

Every morning you just write out three pages by hand ideally of just whatever is on your mind.
这个up主每次在写的时候，首先会在开头上写“Today is going to be a great day”（今天将是美好的一天）如果不知道写什么，就把比如今天是星期几、现在几点、在哪里记录、周围环境等等等等随性记录，大概15-20min，可以先从仅仅3行开始写起。

（我认为比较重要的是：早晨、手写、15-20min。）

Level2: Writing about how you are feeling

表达性写作（expressive writing）

（论文里）write expressively about a past painful event
（up主提到的）Write about any kind of painful, negative or dramatic event.

感恩日记

记录下想要感激的事情（gratitude journaling）
e.g. What are 3 things you’re grateful for?

进阶：「I appreciate …」Exercise

  1. Find someone that you want to express gratitude towards

  2. Pick three qualities that characterize this person

  3. Write about those and a time when they displayed them

这个练习的重点在于勇敢的向对方展示自己写的东西，形成一种互动。

Level3: Write about your next decisions and actions

Prompt 1: Odyssey Plan

问自己3个问题：

  1. What does my life look like 5 years from now if I continue down on the same path?
  2. What does my life look like 5 years from now if I take a completely different path?
  3. What does my life look like if I take a different path but not worry about money or others’ opinions?

Prompt 2: The Wheel of Life

Prompt 3: The 12 Month Celebration

上面这2个在他b站第1个视频（24规划，BV1eN4y1i7ST）里有更详细的介绍，之后也会做整理，这里不再赘述。

Prompt 4: Fear Setting Exercise

问自己5个问题：

  1. What is the worst thing that will happen if I do the thing that I fear doing?
  2. What can I do to prevent each of the worst things from happening?
  3. If the worst case scenario happened, what can I do to repair it?
  4. What are the benefits of an attempt or partial success?
  5. If I don’t do the thing that scares me, what will my life look like in 6 months, 1 year, and 3 years?

Prompt 5: Solomon’s Paradox

简单描述一下：想象一个年龄更大一点的自己（比如85岁的自己），和这个“另一个我”进行对话，询问这个自己现在应该怎么做。（因为可能现在在纠结的事情在宏观层面可能根本不算事）、
